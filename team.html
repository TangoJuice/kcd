Dr. Kyleen Chen

  • Graduated UT Austin in 1994 with a Bachelor of Art degree in Biology

  • Earned D.D.S. from Baylor College of Dentistry, Dallas, TX, in 1998

  • Following dental school, worked one year as a dentist in Austin

  • Moved to and stayed in California from 2000 to 2005

  • While in California, worked as an associate dentist in both family and childrens dentistry. Also, was active volunteering in underprivileged communities, promoting dental hygiene education and participated as a lecturer in dental assistant accreditation program.

  • Returned to Austin Texas in 2006 to establish KC Dental.

  • Nitrous Oxide Sedation

  • Dental Implant Restoration

  • Certified 3M Mini Implant Dentist

  • Invisalign certified dentist provider

  • Completed over 150 CE hours of Straight Wire Orthodontics

Dr. Sid Gupta

  • Obtained his dental degree from Manipal College of Dental Surgery, India, in 2002

  • Two years of advanced research at the State University of New York, Stony Brook in Oral Biology and Pathology understanding the factors which cause periodontal disease (gum disease)

  • Attended the University of Texas Dental Branch at Houston for his Master's and specialist training in Periodontics and Implant dentistry. He has extensive surgical experience in placing implants and other advanced surgical techniques. He also provides various sedation dentistry techniques for anxious patients including enteral (oral) and IV sedation

  • Dr. Gupta, his wife, and a son, currently live in Northwest Austin

Dr. Steven Hansen

  • Graduated U.C.L.A in 1976 with a Bachelor's Degree

  • Attended U.S.C. Dental School on a full scholarship, earning his D.D.S in 1980

  • Following his certification, Dr. Hansen practiced general dentistry for the United States Air Force for 11 years

  • In 1991, attained his Certification of Endodontic from Wilford Hall Medical Center, San Antonio

  • Served as Chairman of the Endodontic Dept. at Wilford Hall Medical Center, Air Force's premier medical and dental center

  • Held positions as Program Director for the graduate Endodontic Residency Program

  • Engaged as Special Consultant to the Surgeon General in Endodontics

  • Following his military retirement, He served on the Board of Directors for the Alameda Dental Society

  • Dr. Hansen is Board Certified by the American Board of Endodontics, has held clinical faculty positions at the University of Texas and has authored / co-authored numerous scientific publications.
