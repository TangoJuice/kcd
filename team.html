Dr. Court Wilson

  • Graduated from the University of Houston in 2000 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology

  • Earned D.D.S. from the University of Texas Health Science Center San Antonio in 2008

  • Completed a 1 year GPR (general pracice residency) at the University of Texas Health Science Center San Antonio in 2009. In the residency, Dr. Wilson: extracted wisdom teeth, performed molar endodontics, performed IV sedation, treated medically complex patients, placed implants, restored implants, and practiced cosmetic dentistry. Dr. Wilson was on call for the University Hospital in San Antonio where he would treat facial swellings and trauma to the oral/facial structures.

  • Dr. Wilson moved to Austin in 2009 and is active in many dental societies. Dr. Wilson enjoys continuing education, and he is pursuing the Fellowship in the Academy of General Dentistry.

Dr. Steven Hansen

  • Graduated U.C.L.A in 1976 with a Bachelor's Degree

  • Attended U.S.C. Dental School on a full scholarship, earning his D.D.S in 1980

  • Following his certification, Dr. Hansen practiced general dentistry for the United States Air Force for 11 years

  • In 1991, attained his Certification of Endodontic from Wilford Hall Medical Center, San Antonio

  • Served as Chairman of the Endodontic Dept. at Wilford Hall Medical Center, Air Force's premier medical and dental center

  • Held positions as Program Director for the graduate Endodontic Residency Program

  • Engaged as Special Consultant to the Surgeon General in Endodontics

  • Following his military retirement, He served on the Board of Directors for the Alameda Dental Society

  • Dr. Hansen is Board Certified by the American Board of Endodontics, has held clinical faculty positions at the University of Texas and has authored / co-authored numerous scientific publications.
